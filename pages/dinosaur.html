Zigong Dinosaur Museum

The Zigong Dinosaur Museum, located in Zigong, Sichuan Province, is one of the world's largest dinosaur museums. It was built on the site of a rich fossil excavation, where numerous dinosaur fossils have been discovered since 1972.

The museum houses an impressive collection of dinosaur fossils, including complete skeletons of various species that lived during the Jurassic period. Visitors can explore exhibits showcasing the diversity of prehistoric life and learn about the geological history of the Zigong area.

This world-renowned museum not only serves as a center for paleontological research but also offers an engaging educational experience for visitors of all ages, making it a must-visit destination for dinosaur enthusiasts and anyone interested in natural history.
