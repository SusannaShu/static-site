Zigong Famous Dishes

自贡冷吃兔 (Zigong Cold Rabbit)

自贡冷吃兔 is a famous dish from Zigong, featuring tender rabbit meat prepared with a unique blend of spices. The dish is typically served cold, hence the name "cold rabbit." It's known for its spicy and numbing flavors, characteristic of Sichuan cuisine.

富顺豆花 (Fushun Tofu Pudding)

富顺豆花 is a popular dessert originating from Fushun county in Zigong. It's a soft, silky tofu pudding often served with sweet syrup or savory toppings. The dish is known for its smooth texture and delicate flavor, making it a beloved local treat.
