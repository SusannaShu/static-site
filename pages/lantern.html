Zigong Lantern Festival

The Zigong Lantern Festival is a dazzling display of traditional Chinese lantern art, featuring intricate designs and vibrant colors that light up the night sky.

Visitors from all over the world come to Zigong to experience the magical atmosphere created by thousands of handcrafted lanterns during this annual festival.

The festival often features themed lantern displays, such as these illuminated dinosaur lanterns, combining Zigong's rich paleontological history with traditional lantern craftsmanship.

The Zigong Lantern Festival transforms the city into a radiant wonderland, showcasing the skill and creativity of local artisans while preserving an important cultural tradition.
