About Zigong

Zigong is a prefecture-level city located in the Sichuan Province of southwest China. It is situated approximately:

  • 170 km (106 miles) southeast of Chengdu, the provincial capital
  • 260 km (162 miles) northeast of Chongqing

Climate

Zigong has a subtropical monsoon climate characterized by:

  • Mild winters and hot summers
  • Average annual temperature of around 17°C (63°F)
  • Annual rainfall of approximately 1,000 mm (39 inches)
  • High humidity throughout the year

The city experiences a long frost-free period, making it suitable for agriculture. The best time to visit Zigong is during spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) when the weather is most pleasant.
