Zigong Salt Production

Traditional Salt Production in Zigong

Zigong has been famous for its salt production for over 2,000 years. The traditional method of salt production in Zigong involves the following steps:

  1. Brine Extraction: Deep wells are drilled to access underground brine reservoirs.
  2. Brine Lifting: The brine is lifted to the surface using bamboo pipelines and manual labor.
  3. Brine Evaporation: The brine is poured into large, shallow pans and heated to evaporate the water.
  4. Salt Crystallization: As the water evaporates, salt crystals form and are collected.
  5. Drying and Packaging: The salt is dried and packaged for distribution.

This ancient technique has been refined over centuries, making Zigong salt highly prized for its purity and flavor.
